### 1) Clair Obscur: Expedition 33

⭐ **PS5 Store:** 4.85/5 | **Critics:** 92.0/100 | **Community:** 9.5/10

**Primary:** Single Player Only | **Mode:** Solo Focused | **Viewpoint:** Third Person | **Difficulty:** Hard | **Adjustable Difficulty:** Yes

**Theme:** Mortality, hope, and resistance against inevitable loss.

**Story:** Expedition 33 sets out to stop the Paintress, whose yearly ritual erases entire age groups, in a desperate final mission across a surreal Belle Époque-inspired world.

**Gameplay:** Reactive turn-based combat with real-time defensive and timing mechanics, character synergies, exploration, and cinematic boss encounters.

**Genres:** Turn-based RPG, fantasy drama, cinematic adventure

[Open PlayStation Store](https://store.playstation.com/sv-se/concept/10008503)

### 2) Baldur's Gate 3

### 3) The Witcher 3: The Wild Hunt

### 4) Red Dead Redemption 2

⭐ **PS5 Store:** 4.75/5 | **Critics:** 97.0/100 | **Community:** 9.0/10

**Primary:** Single Player Only | **Mode:** Solo Focused | **Viewpoint:** Third Person | **Difficulty:** Medium | **Adjustable Difficulty:** No

### 5) Cyberpunk 2077

**Primary:** Single Player Only | **Mode:** Solo Focused | **Viewpoint:** First Person | **Difficulty:** Medium | **Adjustable Difficulty:** Yes
